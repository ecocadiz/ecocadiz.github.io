INTRODUCTION

Japanese Incareratation During WWII : Lasting Effects

My project aims to show a part of the Japanese internment story, specifically to highlight where/when these populations were mobilized, and show how those interned faced long-lasting setbacks upon their reintegration into society after the war. The dataset I used to create my rough visualization is called 'JapaneseAmericanPopulation_1940_1945.csv'. In this CSV file contains information on the number of evacuees known to have returned to west coast states in 1945 compared to 1940. In my visualization it shows west coast states; California, Oregon and Washington japanese and japanese-american populations from 1940 and 1945, as well as the percentage of those who returned. No data cleaning was necessary as it has already been cleaned prior to my use of it.
